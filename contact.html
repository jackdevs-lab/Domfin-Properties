Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a property viewing?

You can schedule a viewing by filling out the contact form above, calling our office, or using the "Schedule Viewing" button on any property listing.

What areas do you specialize in?

We specialize in upscale neighborhoods in Nairobi including Westlands, Kilimani, Kileleshwa, Lavington, Karen, and Runda.

Do you offer property management services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive property management services including tenant placement, rent collection, and maintenance coordination.

What are your commission rates?

Our commission rates are competitive and vary based on the type and value of the property. Contact us for specific details.
